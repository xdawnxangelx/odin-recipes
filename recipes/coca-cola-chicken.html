Coca Cola Chicken

chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken breasts
  • 1 can of coca cola
  • 1/2 cup ketchup or tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • italian seasoning
  • 1 onion
  1. trim chicken breasts, place chicken in the bottom of the crockpot
  2. chop onion and garlic, spread out over the top of chicken
  3. add coke down the sides, add ketchup over top
  4. cook 6 hours on low
  5. serve
