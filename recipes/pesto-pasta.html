Pesto Pasta

an italian-cajun fusion pesto pasta dish, with chicken and andouille sausage

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes penne pasta
  • salt, to taste
  • creole seasoning, to taste
  • 1 jar pesto sauace
  • precooked chicken
  • andouille sausage, sliced, quartered
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 orange bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • garlic, to taste
  • butter or olive oil
  1. chop garlic, saute in olive oil or butter on low heat in large pan
  2. chop onions, add to pan when garlic becomes fragrant
  3. salt pot of water, bring to a boil, add pasta, cook 7 minutes
  4. slice sausage, quarter
  5. chop bell peppers
  6. drain pasta, add pesto and mix
  7. stir in bell peppers, chicken and sausage
  8. cook 10-15 minutes on medium heat
  9. serve
