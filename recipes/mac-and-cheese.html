Macaroni and Cheese

macaroni and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1lb macaroni
  • 1 stick melted butter
  • 4 cups extra-sharp cheddar cheese, divided
  • 2 cups colby jack-cheddar blend
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F and grease large deep baking dish with butter
  2. cook macaoni according to package directions in a pot of boiling salted water
  3. add butter, salt and pepper to cooked pasta
  4. stir in two cups of cheddar cheese
  5. add half of the macaroni mixture to baking dish
  6. sprinkle colby-cheddar cheese blend over pasta mixture, top with another layer of pasta
  7. in a medium bowl, beat together eggs and milk then pour evenly over pasta
  8. cover baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25-35 minutes
  9. turn on oven broiler, top with remaining cheddar and colby jack
  10. broil until golden, it will firm up as it cools
