New York Style Cheesecake

a decadent full bodied cheesecake

Graham Cracker Crust Ingredients

  • 6 oz graham crackers
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Filling Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs room temperature cream cheese
  • 6 whole large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1.75 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/8 tbsp salt
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  1. preheat oven to 325 degree F
  2. grease bottom of springform pan with 1 tbsp melted butter
  3. pulse graham crackers in food processor
  4. mix in 6 tbsp melted butter and 1 tbsp granulated sugar until the mixture takes on a wet appearance
  5. press graham cracker mixture into springform pan
  6. bake for 12 minutes or until fragrant and golden brown, remove from oven
  7. preheat oven to 500 degrees F
  8. once the pan has cooled, use half tablespoon to butter the sides
  9. cut the cream cheese into small chunks, put into mixing bowl
  10. beat cream cheese on low until smooth, about 2-3 minutes
  11. add 1/8 tbsp salt and about a third of the sugar, 1 tbsp of four, beat until integrated, scrape down sides
  12. add another third of the sugar and 1 tbsp of flour, and continue to mix
  13. add final third of sugar and last tbsp of flour and mix in
  14. add 2 tsp lemon juice and 1 tsp vanilla extract and mix
  15. scrape down sides and add 1/2 cup heavy cream
  16. add egg yolks and mix until they are blended in
  17. add 3 whole eggs, mix until they have been completely mixed in
  18. scrape down sides, beat final 3 eggs in
  19. pour filling into cooled springform pan on top of the crust
  20. lift pan an inch or two above counter, drop it to bring trapped bubbles to the surface
  21. place springform pan onto a sheetpan and slide into middle of preheated oven
  22. cook cheesecake for a full 10 minutes at 500 degrees, then drop the temperature to 200 degrees
  23. allow cheesecake to bake as oven gradually reduces temperature (do not open oven)
  24. bake cheesecake until the center of the cake registers 150 degrees F (about 1 hour, 40 minutes
  25. remove cake from oven, place on cooling rack for 10 minutes, run paring knife around the rim to release cake from walls of the pan
  26. cover with a large bowl to reduce heat gradually (reduces likelihood of a cracked cake), cool three hours
  27. wrap pan tightly in plastic wrap and chill for a minimum of five hours in the fridge
