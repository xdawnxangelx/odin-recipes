Peanut Butter Cookies

the best peanut butter cookies that ever existed

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • peanut butter chips, to taste (optional)
  • chocolate chips, to taste (optional)
  • butterscotch chips, to taste (optional)
  1. Mix sugars, peanut butter, shortening, egg and butter in a large bowl
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients
  3. Place in refrigerator, covered, for about 2 hours or until firm
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  5. Shape dough into 1 1/4 inch balls, place about 3 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Flatten in crisscross pattern with tines of fork dipped in sugar (to prevent fork from sticking to dough)
  7. Bake 9-10 minutes or until light golden brown
  8. Wait 5 minutes before transferring to wire rack to cool
