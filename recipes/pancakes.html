Pancakes

just your basic pancake recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  1. preheat over medium-high heat
  2. mix dry and wet separately
  3. pour wet into dry, mix until smooth
  4. pour 1/4 cup of batter into the pan
  5. brown both sides of pancake
  6. serve
